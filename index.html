Frequently Asked Questions

How does textpert.net work?

It's simple! Just text us what service you need to 480-555-1212. We'll connect you with qualified service providers in your area. You can communicate directly with them via text, receive video solutions, participate in live streaming consultations, or schedule in-person visits.

Is there a fee to use textpert.net?

The service is completely free for consumers. There are no hidden fees or charges. You pay only for the services you agree to with service providers.

How are service providers vetted?

We verify all service providers before they can join our platform. This includes checking licenses, insurance, and business credentials. We also collect and display customer reviews to help you make informed decisions.

What types of service providers can I find?

textpert.net connects you with a wide range of service providers, including home services (plumbing, electrical, HVAC, etc.) and life services (coaching, fitness, nutrition, etc.). Our directory is constantly growing to meet your needs.

How do I become a service provider on textpert.net?

To join as a service provider, click the "Become a Service Provider" button and complete the application process. You'll need to provide your business information, credentials, and service details. Once approved, you can start receiving leads and building your profile.

How is pricing determined?

textpert.net doesn't set prices for services. Service providers set their own rates, and you negotiate directly with them. This ensures transparent pricing and allows you to find services that fit your budget.

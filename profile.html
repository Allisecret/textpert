About Elite Plumbing & Heating

Elite Plumbing & Heating has been serving the Phoenix metropolitan area since 2005. We are a family-owned and operated business committed to providing exceptional plumbing, heating, and HVAC services to homeowners and businesses alike.

Our team of highly trained technicians brings decades of combined experience to every job, big or small. We pride ourselves on delivering reliable service, quality workmanship, and fair pricing on every project we undertake.

What sets us apart is our innovative approach to customer service. Through our text-based communication system, we provide faster response times, more efficient service, and better overall experience for our clients.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional plumbing, heating, and HVAC services that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, quality, and integrity.

Our Story

Elite Plumbing & Heating was founded in 2005 by master plumber David Morgan after 15 years of working for larger companies in the industry. David saw an opportunity to create a more personalized service experience that would truly address homeowners' needs.

Starting with just two service vans and a small team, Elite quickly gained a reputation for reliability and excellent workmanship. By 2010, we had expanded to include heating and cooling services, and today we operate a fleet of 12 service vehicles with a team of 18 technicians.

In 2022, we revolutionized our business by introducing our innovative text-based communication system, allowing customers to get help faster and easier than ever before. This commitment to innovation continues to drive our business forward.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, pricing, and policies. Can't find what you're looking for? Text us directly for a quick response.

How does your texting service work?

Our texting service allows you to communicate with our team directly via SMS. Simply text your plumbing, heating, or HVAC questions or issues to 480-555-1212, and one of our specialists will respond promptly. You can describe your problem, send photos or videos, and even receive video solutions from our technicians. It's a faster, more convenient way to get expert help.

What are your service hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Saturday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. For emergencies, we offer 24/7 service, including Sundays and holidays. Our texting service is available at all times, though response times may be quicker during regular business hours.

How do you determine pricing?

We provide upfront, transparent pricing before beginning any work. For standard services, we have set pricing which you can find on our Services page. For more complex projects, we'll provide a detailed estimate after assessing your specific needs. We never add hidden fees or surprise charges after the work is completed.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with strong warranties. All service repairs carry a 1-year warranty on parts and labor. New installations come with a 5-year warranty on labor, in addition to any manufacturer warranties on equipment. Our Home Maintenance Plan members receive extended warranty coverage on all services.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

For true plumbing, heating, or AC emergencies, we typically respond within 1-2 hours. Our average emergency response time is 90 minutes. When you text or call our emergency line, we prioritize your request and dispatch the nearest available technician to your location as quickly as possible.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), cash, checks, and digital payment methods including Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Venmo. For larger projects, we also offer financing options with approved credit.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Yes, all our technicians are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. They undergo extensive background checks, drug testing, and continuous training to ensure they meet our high standards of professionalism and expertise. We're fully insured with general liability coverage of $2 million and complete workers' compensation coverage.
